Frequently Asked Questions

Your car Not Accelerating or Slow in Pickup?

If you are finding a slow acceleration or rather pathetic pickup - reason could be ranging to number of factors. But, for self-diagnosis you could check in - when was the last time - Air Filter & Fuel Filter were replaced, Spark Plug & Fuel Injectors were cleaned.For very heavy usage cars - Engine Decarbonizing is done to remove Carbon deposits.

Low Mileage, Poor Performance, Excessive Smoke?

Most Likely Fuel Filter Assembly be clogged and may need replacement or Worse need Expensive Turbo Replacement. Though - Dealer claim in Turbo Issue in Diesel Cars - but Turbocharger does not fail in like this - Best is to go in with Diagnostic Scanning Check to rule out Turbo Issue.

Faulty Starter?

If it’s hard to start your car you may be starting to hear a click before the car starts – if this is the case it could be time to replace your starter motor. If it goes the car will no longer start so it needs fixing sooner rather than later.

Car Starting to Vibrate or Jerk?

It possibly happens due to Misaligned Tyres, Incorrect Tyre pressure giving a feel of vibration on potholes, Get Wheel Alignment and Balancing done and check Tyre Pressure. If Heavy Jerk happening after filling Fuel then possibly Fuel Contamination where Fuel filter are clogged or else your car Fuel Injector gone bad.

Are your car Brakes Squeaking/Grinding?

The brake system will start to wear out just like any other moving part you have in the car. Brakes are vital – they are essential to make sure you can stop safety so any kind of brake related problems, including unusual noise or a soft brake pedal, should be taken care of by a professional mechanic at the earliest possible opportunity. There are some minor issues that can cause brakes to squeak or squeal but once you start to notice grinding you definitely need to get them replaced.

Noise from Engine?

Low Engine Oil or Heavily Clogged Oil Filter can cause Noise from Engine. Do check if Engine Mount is broken as a probability for it too, Loose or Misaligned Timing Belt may also cause noise from Engine. If you hearing some kind of chan-chan sound and goes off while pressing clutch then it can be from Clutch Bearing which is an inexpensive component and cost around Rs 600 to 1000 in most of the mass market cars. Caution: Do not Drive when Engine Oil is below the Minimum Mark - it may Seize in the Engine causing very Expensive repair work.

AC not cooling your car?

Most Common reason is either the Condenser and AC Evaporator Coils might be full of Dirt - may need cleaning or AC Gas might be leaked or finished, the gas needs to be refilled. In Cars with = Cabin Air Filter installed - it could be full of dust and needs immediate replacement.
